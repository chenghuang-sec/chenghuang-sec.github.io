I am a tenure-track associate professor at School of Computer Science, Fudan University. My main research area is Data Security & Privacy for AI Applications.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo, ON, Canada, in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Xuemin (Sherman) Shen. Before joining Fudan University, I were a Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo from 2020 to 2023.
