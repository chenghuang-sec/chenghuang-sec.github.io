I am a tenure-track associate professor at School of Computer Science, Fudan University, Shanghai, China. My main research area is Data Security & Privacy for AI.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Waterloo(Canada), under the supervision of Prof. Xuemin (Sherman) Shen. Before joining the Fudan University, I was a Research Assistant in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the Nanyang Technological University(Singapore) from 2015 to 2016, and was a PostDoc Research Fellow in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Waterloo(Canada) from 2020 to 2023.
